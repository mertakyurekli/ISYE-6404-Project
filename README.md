## Data sources & Links

### Consumer Price Index: The Consumer Price Index (CPI) consists of a family of indexes that measure price change experienced by urban consumers. This data is from FRED. 
https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/CPIAUCSL

### Unemployment Rate: The unemployment rate represents the number of unemployed people as a percentage of the labor force (the labor force is the sum of the employed and unemployed). This data is from FRED. 
https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/UNRATE
### Crude Oil Prices: West Texas Intermediate (WTI) is a light, sweet crude oil that serves as one of the main global oil benchmarks. 
https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/MCOILWTICO
### CPI: Used Cars: Measures affordability of Used Automobiles. This data is from FRED. 
https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/CUSR0000SETA02
### CPI: New Cars: Measures affordability of Newly Purchased Automobiles. This data is from FRED. 
https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/CUSR0000SETA01
### Total Vehicle Sales: The number of units of vehicles sold per month in the United States. 
https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/TOTALSA
### Real Estate ETF: State Street SPDR Real Estate ETF (proxy for Real Estate market). 
https://finance.yahoo.com/quote/XLRE?p=XLRE
### Real Disposable Personal Income: Per Capita: Income remaining after deduction of taxes and other mandatory charges, at a per person level. 
https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/A229RX0
